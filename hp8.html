Other facts

Although the Deathly Hallows is the official last book of the series, a play was written as a glimspe 19 years into the future, which stars Harry's and Draco's kids. It was also written as a screen play, and published. It is known as Harry Potter and the Cursed Child.

All big fans of the Harry Potter series are called Potterheads, sort of like Voldemort's followers are called Death Eaters.
