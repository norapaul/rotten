Harry Potter - The Series

Harry Potter is a popular children's series. The story is about an orphaned boy named Harry Potter, and his two best friends ; Hermione Granger and Ronald Weasley who all attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Throughout the books, Harry and his friends fight against Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal and take over all of the wizarding and non magical population.

This is a picture of the golden trio. To the left is Ronald, right is Hermione, and center is none other than Harry Potter himself.

Harry Potter has 7 books. The names can be found on the right, in the sidebar. The 8th story, The Cursed Child, was a play set 19 years after the 7th book. There have been many replays all around the world, and JK Rowling has even released a screenplay.

The book titles are...

  • Chamber of Secrets
  • Prisoner of Askaban
  • Goblet of Fire
  • Order of Phoenix
  • Half Blood Prince
  • Deathly Hallows
