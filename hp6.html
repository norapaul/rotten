Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince

As Death Eaters wreak havoc in both Muggle and Wizard worlds, Hogwarts is no longer a safe place for students. Though Harry suspects there are new dangers lurking within the castle walls, Dumbledore is more determined than ever on preparing the young wizard for the final battle with Voldemort. tragedy looms, and Hogwarts may never be the same again.

Interesting Fact!

Young Voldemort/Tom Riddle is played by Hero Fiennes-Tiffin. Fiennes-Tiffin is the nephew of Ralph Fiennes, who plays adult Voldemort/Tom Riddle. How cool is that?
